The Back Benchers.

Back benchers: This is a friends group of ISC at batch 22. Some of us became friends at the time of birth. And some of us at the school life. But till now, we all are together and we are all very close friends. We are always together when we need each other. Sometimes we may fight and quarrel with each other. But it makes us more close friends. Our hope, we may stay like this till the last breathe of each of us.

We have different kinds of aims and destinations in our lives. But at the end we all are always together, whatever happen to us. And we hope, we will be always together. Even if we get lost in our works, we will at the last find each other and recall those old memories. This is the “back benchers”
